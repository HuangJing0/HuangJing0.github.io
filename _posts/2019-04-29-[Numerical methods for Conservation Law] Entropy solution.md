>Entropy solution(熵解)

### 熵解定义
The entropy solution to $u_t+f(u)_x=0$ is the limit of $u^{\epsilon}$, the solution to  $$u^{\epsilon}_t+f(u^{\epsilon})_x=\epsilon u^{\epsilon}_{xx},$$ as $\epsilon\rightarrow0.$

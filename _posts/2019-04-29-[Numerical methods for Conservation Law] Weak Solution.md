>Weak solution(弱解)

### 弱解定义
初始条件为$u(x,0)=u_0$的守恒律方程$u_t+f(u)_x=0$，其弱解$u(x,t)$满足：对于任意的测试函数(test function)$\phi\in C_c^1(\mathbf{R}\times\mathbf{R})$,
\[\int_0^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}[\phi_t u +\phi_xf(u)]dxdt=-\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\phi(x,0)u_0(x)dx.\]
